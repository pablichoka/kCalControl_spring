A tool for those who do not want to think what to eat to get in shape.

kCal Control is an application which calculates the amount of kCal that you need to gain weight, maintain it or lose it providing a daily menu so that the user do not have to think what to eat. Always with your favourites ingredients and in a healthy way!

Evaluating

First of all, you can measure the amount of kCal per day that your body needs depending on your weight objectives and the number of days that you do exercise (estimate 1 hour per day).

Selecting

When the app knows your needs, then is the moment to start planning your dishes. You can select your preferences if they are non-negotiable. After that, you can click the button and you will get your magic daily menu.

Reviewing

Finally, you can note your results on the app to keep a tracking of them, the percentage of fats, carbs and proteins that you have eaten and getting a recommendation from the algorithm to improve your diet if you have particular dishes or if it is necessary.
